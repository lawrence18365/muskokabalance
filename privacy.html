Privacy Policy

How We Protect Your Information

We respect your trust. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when interacting with Muskoka Balance.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect what we need to fulfill your order or respond to your inquiries:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Order information including product selections, payment confirmations, and delivery preferences.
  • Voluntary messages you send via email, social platforms, or website forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data allows us to provide a smooth, personal experience. Typical uses include:

  • Processing and confirming purchases, deliveries, or refunds.
  • Responding to questions, feedback, or wellness support requests.
  • Sending important updates about your order, policy changes, or product availability.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are handled securely through trusted providers. We never store your full credit card number on our servers.

4. Data Retention and Security

We keep personal data only as long as necessary to comply with legal requirements or to support you as a customer. Access is limited to team members who need it to fulfill orders, and we use secure systems to protect it from unauthorized access.

5. Your Choices

  • Request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to update or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of non-essential emails at any time.

Reach us at muskokabalance@gmail.com for any privacy requests.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as our services evolve. The latest version is always posted on this page, with an updated effective date.
