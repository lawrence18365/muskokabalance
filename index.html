The ritual

How Grounded enters the day

We keep the practice deliberate: a single scoop, cool water, a quiet breath. The goal is composure, not stimulation.

03 quiet gestures.

01

Measure

Seven grams of Grounded, level scoop. Nothing added, nothing flavored.

Scoop + 250ml spring water
02

Suspend

Whisk or shake gently until the botanicals lift. The color should hold a muted jade.

03

Sip + breathe

Drink slowly within five minutes. Let it follow coffee or stand alone before breakfast.

Field notes

Assurances behind every jar

Grounded is a single formula, but its quality rests on dozens of quiet decisions— the farms we choose, the slow milling, the patience in testing. Consider this our ledger.

Sourcing

Ontario + coastal botanicals

Greens harvested in Muskoka, northern Ontario and the Pacific Northwest, certified organic, never bought on commodity exchanges. We work with six farms season after season.

Milling

Stone-ground in small lots

Botanicals are dried below 45°C and milled over 18 hours for a fine, spoon-ready powder. No carriers, no “natural flavor”. Just the plants.

Verification

Third-party lab certificates

Every batch is screened for heavy metals, microbial load, and potency. We share the data—no summary cards, just the actual reports.
