About / Founder

Kali Smith

Founder of Muskoka Balance. Building something clean, simple, and honest.

I'm Kali Smith, the founder of Muskoka Balance. I created this brand because I wanted something clean, simple, and honest—something I couldn't find anywhere else.

As I've been studying holistic nutrition and learning more about the way the body actually works, one thing became obvious to me: most wellness products look premium on the outside but don't match that quality inside. Too many fillers. Too much marketing. Not enough transparency. Nothing felt aligned with the standard I hold for myself or the people I want to help.

So I decided to build my own solution—something grounded, intentional, and designed with purpose.

My first product, Grounded, reflects everything Muskoka means to me: nature, simplicity, and quiet luxury. I focus on clean ingredients, straightforward formulations, and a premium experience from start to finish. Nothing hidden. Nothing unnecessary. Just quality that actually speaks for itself.

Muskoka Balance isn't just a product line for me—it's the lifestyle I live every day. I believe in habits, structure, routine, and doing things in a way that supports your long-term wellbeing. I'm not interested in overcomplicated trends or unrealistic approaches. I want people to feel strong, balanced, and grounded in their own lives.

I'm building this brand intentionally and slowly, because the details matter to me. Every choice—from sourcing, to packaging, to how the brand feels—is part of a bigger vision I'm committed to bringing to life.

If you have questions or want support, I respond personally. Muskoka Balance is my work, my passion, and my future—and I'm proud to share it with you.

Kali Smith

Founder, Muskoka Balance
