About / Founder

Kali Smith

Herbalist, sports nutritionist, and Muskoka local.

I am Kali Smith, owner, clinical nutritionist, and perennial note-taker behind Muskoka Balance. Years spent guiding elite athletes and postpartum clients taught me that people crave focused protocols, not commodity greens. Our studio in Bracebridge keeps me close to the growers, the weather, and the cedar-lined trails that inform every blend.

Grounded is treated like a culinary project: botanicals are shade-dried, milled in cold drums, rested to stabilize volatiles, and dosed to clinical standards before a jar leaves the studio. I sign off on every lot and log sourcing, assays, and sensory notes in the same ledger my grandmother used for preserves.

Rituals matter more than marketing. I spend as much time designing daily scripts, onboarding families, and briefing practitioners as I do filling canisters. If you need dosing guidance or travel sachets, you reach me—not a call center.

Kali Smith

Owner, Formulator, Clinical Nutritionist
